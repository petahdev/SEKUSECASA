Service Details

Our Sunday service begins every week at 10 AM, preceded by the Rosary prayer. It is a time for reflection, prayer, and fellowship, fostering spiritual growth in our community.

The magical Choir

Join the SECASA Magical Choir and lift your voice in praise! If you have a gift for singing and a heart for worship, we welcome you to be part of this blessed ministry and sing for the Lord.

Catholic men association

The Catholic Men Association embodies strong, unwavering faith, fostering unity and leadership. We are committed to building a solid foundation of brotherhood while focusing on the spiritual growth and service to the Church.

Catholic ladies association

The Catholic Ladies Association stands as a pillar of faith, nurturing compassion and service. We are dedicated to fostering sisterhood and spiritual development, united in our mission to serve the Church and community.

Charity work

Charity work reflects our core commitment to helping others, while the details lie in the acts of kindness that uplift lives. Every effort, big or small, helps spread love and hope throughout the community.

Catechism

Catechism is the heart of our faith, and our catechism classes offer a welcoming space to learn and grow spiritually. Join us to deepen your understanding of the Church’s teachings and strengthen your connection with God.

How to Grow in Faith

01. Believe
Embrace the core beliefs of the Catholic faith, including the teachings of the Church and the sacraments.
02. Pray
Develop a regular prayer life, communicating with God through personal prayers, the Rosary, and participation in Mass.
03. Learn
Study the Scriptures and the Catechism of the Catholic Church to deepen your understanding of faith and morals.
04. Serve
Get involved in community service and parish activities, living out your faith by helping others and sharing your gifts.
01. Commit
Fully engage in the life of the Church by participating in the sacraments, particularly the Eucharist, and fostering a relationship with God and your faith community.
